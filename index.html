01 · THE CATALYST

Feb 2026: the moment CHZ flipped

CHZ spent five years as an inflation-only ecosystem currency with no value-accrual mechanism, despite Chiliz building the dominant Fan Token franchise in sport. That changed in February 2026, when Chiliz announced that 10% of all Fan Token revenue would be redirected into open-market CHZ buybacks and burns. That single decision converted CHZ from a passive utility token into the primary financial beneficiary of every Fan Token sale, every marketplace trade, and every block produced on the chain.

02 · CUMULATIVE BURN

Tokens removed, forever

Tokenomics 2.0 went live in March 2026 and removed roughly 18 million CHZ from circulation in just 60 days. To put that in context, the previous gas-fee-only burn regime needed multiple years to take an equivalent amount of supply out of the market. The cumulative line below is no longer a slow drift, it is a step change in how value accrues to the token.

03 · MONTHLY VOLUME

Burn rate is accelerating

March 2026 was the step-function moment for CHZ, and April topped it. Burn velocity is no longer dependent on gas fees alone. Every Fan Token sale, every marketplace trade, and every transaction on the network now feeds directly into open-market buybacks. The pace is compounding, not flattening.

04 · INFLATION SCHEDULE

Issuance is decaying

CHZ issuance follows a programmed decay schedule, falling from 8.8% in 2026 to a 1.88% terminal rate by 2040. Every year, the bar that burns need to clear in order to flip CHZ into net deflation drops materially. Burns do not need to scale exponentially to win this race. They just need to keep compounding while issuance grinds lower.

05 · NET SUPPLY PATH

Issuance vs. burns

On Pylon's central case, the burn curve crosses the issuance curve by 2030. From that point forward, CHZ supply contracts every single year. The combination of a decaying inflation schedule and a buyback engine that scales with ecosystem revenue is what makes the long-term math work for holders.

06 · ANNOUNCEMENT IMPACT

The chart that says it all

Chiliz announced the Tokenomics 2.0 buyback program on February 10, 2026, and the market began pricing it in within weeks. By March, the first burns had executed, and within 60 days monthly burn volume had scaled fifty times above its prior baseline. CHZ rerated approximately +50% in the same window. This is no longer a thesis being argued, it is being executed in real time.

07 · FAN TOKEN PLAY

Win burns. Loss mints.

Fan Token Play affects club tokens like $BAR, $PSG, and $JUV, not CHZ itself. Match wins burn the club's Fan Tokens, losses mint them, and net supply changes are capped at a 75% lifetime reduction per token. The relevance to CHZ is indirect but powerful: anything that drives Fan Token engagement also drives the revenue base that funds CHZ buybacks. This is the demand-side mechanism that keeps the supply-side flywheel turning.
